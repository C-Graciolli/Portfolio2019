Fashion Flashback

Explore some of fashion’s biggest comebacks and the styles that defined the decades. From the miniskirts of the swinging sixties to the double-denim nineties, see how today’s hottest trends made it to the mainstream in the ultimate fashion throwback. Flip the pictures to find out more about your favourite fashions.

60

1960

17-year-old English beauty Jean Shrimpton first features on the front of Vogue.

61

1961

Breakfast at Tiffany's sees Audrey Hepburn's Givenchy little black dress take the world by storm, becoming possibly the most famous dress of all time.

63

1963

The bikini is first featured in mainstream entertainment and is worn by Annette Funicello in William Asher's teen musical-turned-film series, Beach Party.

64

1964

French designer André Courrèges launches his 'Space Age' collection, featuring streamlined geometric styling, mini skirts and futuristic fabrics.

69

1969

Following their adoption at the beginning of the decade, the women’s trousers rose alongside equality in the Sixties from above the ankle trousers to mid-thigh shorts, voicing social parity in a stylish statement.

70

1970

Straight from the catwalk to the beach, the larger than life floppy hat was the unisex hippy accessory of choice in the ‘70s. Commonly called a field or sun hat because of its superior shading properties.

71

1971

Yves Saint Laurent’s nostalgic “Collection 40” harked back to flea market fashion. The designer himself stated that he wanted to shock, and that sometimes it takes the ‘ugliest show in town’ to change the face of fashion.

74

1974

Malcolm McLaren and Vivienne Westwood controversially rename their King’s Road boutique ‘SEX’ – the shop to define punk fashion

76

1976

Farrah Fawcett adorns walls worldwide in the iconic red all-in-one swimsuit poster that propelled her to stardom, going on to be cast in Charlie's Angels and becoming a worldwide sensation.

77

1977

Diane Keaton caused a stir with her androgynous style in Woody Allen's classic comedy, Annie Hall. She famously wore her own wardrobe onscreen, much to the costume designer's dismay.

78

1978

Grease was the word, with fantastic prom dresses, leather jackets and of course Olivia Newton John's all black outfit and cold-shoulder top keeping Danny hot under the collar.

80

1980

David Bowie's 'Ashes to Ashes' video encapsulates the look of the early 80s 'New Romantic' trend, with Bowie's pale complexion and heavy eye make-up characteristic of the movement.

82

1982

Jane Fonda releases her first exercise video, popularising leg warmers and selling over 1 million VCRs in the process - the video was the highest selling over the next few years.

84

1984

Madonna releases 'Like a Virgin', inspiring a generation of young women with an empowering role model and fashion icon - at one point, Macy's allotted an entire floor area for the sale of clothes styled according to Madonna's fashion.

85

1985

Princess Diana wears perhaps her most famous dress at a Gala Dinner at The White House, named 'The Travolta Dress' after her dance with actor John Travolta.

89

1989

Model Kathy Ireland appears on the Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Edition's 25th Anniversary cover - a cover which has since been awarded 'The Greatest Sports Illustrated Swimsuit Cover Of All Time'.

90

1990

Madonna turned heads during her 'Blonde Ambition' tour where she modeled Jean Paul Gaultier's infamous cone bra design.

92

1992

Kate Moss rose to fame with one of her first campaigns for Calvin Klein setting trends with her androgynous style.

94

1994

The Rachel haircut – The Friends star set the style bar with this do, created by Chris McMillan and made infamous by Jennifer Aniston. It was the most requested celebrity haircut of the '90s.

96

1996

The Spice Girls released their first single ‘Wannabe’, celebrating girl power and individuality. They also brought buffalo boots, union jack dresses and all that body glitter into our lives.

99

1999

Pop princess Britney Spears emerged, setting style standards for the end of the decade with low slung jeans, mini skirts and embellished tank tops.
